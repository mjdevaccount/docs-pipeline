### Fonts: Inter + JetBrains Mono

```
Why Inter?
- Used by Figma, Vercel, GitHub, Stripe
- 100M+ monthly downloads
- 18 weight variants
- Perfect at small sizes (10-14px)
- Free (OFL license)
- Excellent ligatures

Why JetBrains Mono?
- Professional monospace
- Built for code/technical content
- Perfect ligatures (=>, ->, etc.)
- Free (Apache 2.0)
- Used by JetBrains IDEs

Font Stack:
  Body: "Inter", -apple-system, sans-serif
  Code: "JetBrains Mono", monospace
  Size: 13px (from 12px)
```
